# E-commerce Demand Forecasting

## Overview
This project analyzes e-commerce sales data to understand customer behavior,
identify key revenue drivers, and forecast product demand.

## Business Problem
E-commerce companies often struggle with stockouts and overstock due to
inaccurate demand forecasting and limited customer insights.
